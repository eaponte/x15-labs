Frequently Asked Questions

  • How long will it take to build my site?

    Turnaround time is approximately one page per week of a multi-page website or about two sections per week of a single-page website. These are just approximations. The complexity of the website can play a significant role in the development time.

  • What is your payment structure?

    Once we determine the cost to develop your website, 25% is due upfront, the next 50% is due approximately halfway through the development process, and the final 25% is due upon completion of your site. When this last 25% is received, I will send you all files related to your site. At this point, you can upload these files to your preferred hosting company, or I can do that for you at no additional charge.

  • Do you work with WordPress or do back-end development?

    No. I develop websites for small businesses and individuals requiring a static website. Therefore, I do not work with CMS systems such as WordPress or Drupal.

    I also do not develop anything on the back-end. Meaning, I don't develop anything that requires a back-end server to run code or do any type of database management such as running SQL queries or procedures.

  • Do you provide hosting services?

    No. Once I deliver your files, it is up to you to find a hosting provider such as GoDaddy.

    If you have a hosting provider in mind, I can upload your website to that provider at no additional charge.

  • How do I get my domain name?

    Once you decide on a hosting provider, you can purchase your domain name through them at the same time you sign up for web hosting services.

  • Can you get my website to appear on the first page of a Google search?

    While we make every effort to create a standards compliant website, there is never a guarantee as to how Google (or any search engine) will choose to rank any website.

    Website ranking in search engines is determined by many factors including how many visitors visit your site, how many other sites link back to your site, how often your site is mentioned in social media, and how much online marketing you do for your website. You cannot buy your way to the top of search rankings by paying Google either.
